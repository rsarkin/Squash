# Design Specification: Antigravity Module

## 1. Core Concept & Vibe
* **Theme:** Weightless, kinetic, premium performance.
* **Layout Style:** Floating asymmetrical bento-box. Elements should appear unanchored but mathematically balanced using ample whitespace.
* **Animation Logic:** Subtle parallax scrolling. Backgrounds move slower than foreground cards to create depth.

---

## 2. Color Architecture
Pulling from the high-contrast palette, we shift from the heavy forest green to an airy, expansive combination:

* **Primary Background:** Sky/Ice Blue (`#9BCCD0`) - Creates the "open air" illusion.
* **Secondary Cards:** Crisp White (`#FFFFFF`) with a 10% opacity backdrop blur (Glassmorphism).
* **Typography & Icons:** Deep Navy (`#1E4380`) for sharp readability against the blue/white.
* **Energy Accent:** Electric Yellow (`#FBBA16`) reserved exclusively for hover states and interactive SVGs.

---

## 3. Typography
* **Headers (H2, H3):** Poppins. Font-weight: 700 (Bold) and 800 (Extra Bold). Negative letter-spacing (`tracking-tighter`) for that modern, blocky aesthetic.
* **Body Text (P):** Poppins or Inter. Font-weight: 400 (Regular). Generous line-height (`leading-relaxed`) to keep the text feeling light.

---

## 4. Component Structure (Next.js / Tailwind Logic)

### A. The "Hover" Container
A full-width section wrapping the content, utilizing CSS grid for the bento layout. 

* **Tailwind Classes:** `w-full min-h-screen grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-12 gap-6 p-8`

### B. The Left Wing (The Anchor)
A massive, bold typography block that stays sticky while the right side scrolls.

* **Span:** `col-span-1 md:col-span-5`
* **Content:** * A minimalist SVG icon of a feather or a bouncing ball.
  * Headline: "Defy the Drop."
  * Subtext: "Experience courts engineered for maximum shock absorption and explosive liftoff."

### C. The Right Wing (The Floating Bento)
Three staggered cards of varying heights. Applying a subtle drop shadow to simulate floating.

* **Span:** `col-span-1 md:col-span-7 flex flex-col gap-6 pt-12`
* **Card 1 (Tech Specs):** White glass card. Includes 3 SVG data points (e.g., Impact Reduction, Energy Return, Surface Grip).
* **Card 2 (Image Breakout):** A borderless, rounded-corner container for a high-res image of a player mid-jump. 
* **Card 3 (Interactive CTA):** Deep Navy card. Simple text: "Test the Surface." Hovering changes the card border to Electric Yellow.

---

## 5. UI / UX Rules
* **No Clutter:** Maximum of 15 words per floating card. 
* **Shadows:** Use large, highly diffused shadows (e.g., `box-shadow: 0 25px 50px -12px rgba(30, 67, 128, 0.15)`) to make cards pop off the background.
* **Transitions:** All hover states must have a minimum `duration-300` ease-in-out to feel fluid and soft.
